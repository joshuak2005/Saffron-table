Our Story

A humble kitchen, a bold vision, and an unwavering belief that food should make people feel at home.

From a small stove to your table

Saffron Table began in 2018 when chef Lakshmi Iyer, after 15 years working in Michelin-starred kitchens abroad, returned to Chennai with a simple dream: to serve food that tasted like her grandmother's cooking but felt like a fine dining experience.

What started as a 12-seat restaurant in a rented garage has grown into one of the city's most beloved culinary destinations — but the heart of it has never changed. Every recipe is tested dozens of times. Every ingredient is tasted before it enters the kitchen. Every dish is plated as if it were going to a guest of honour.

We believe food is the most honest form of hospitality. When you eat with us, you are eating something that somebody poured themselves into — and we hope you taste that in every bite.

Our Principles

What we stand for

🌿
Farm-Fresh Sourcing

We partner with 14 local farms within 100km of our kitchen. No frozen produce. What's in season is on the menu.

👩‍🍳
Craft Over Convenience

Our stocks are simmered for 12 hours. Our breads are slow-fermented. There are no shortcuts in this kitchen.

♻️
Zero Waste Kitchen

Every vegetable peel has a purpose. All packaging is compostable. Sustainability isn't a marketing line here — it's a practice.

❤️
Community First

We employ from local culinary schools, support food banks, and donate 1% of every order to hunger relief programs.
